About Me

In line with my education and experience, I work by reflecting qualities such as taking part in a team, being able to produce together, and respecting freedom of opinion. I do what I do seriously. I love to improve myself in my field. I am not satisfied with the trainings and courses I have taken, but I am based on continuous research, following the agenda and producing. I love my job, which reflects positively on the work I do and enables me to find more original and innovative ideas. I care about details and small details, and I work devotedly in every moment.

As someone who is aware that developing ideas keeps the mind and therefore the body active, I take care to live in this direction both in my business life and in my personal life. I stay away from uniformity and mediocrity, I am always open to new ideas and different opinions. I closely follow the innovations and developments in my field. I also keep the spirit of self-motivation, entrepreneurship and working together alive in my team.

In order to use my time more efficiently and with high quality, my main principles are to work regularly and in a planned manner, to prioritize my work and to note down what I do. I work with my teammates in an agile and planned manner, taking quick and decisive actions.

I am a successful and preferred software developer in my field. My references and my growing team are the best proof of this. With the trainings I receive, I am constantly improving myself at the point of being the best in my field. I closely follow the innovations and changes made and make my future plans based on innovations suitable for the time.

My vision is to do the best, the highest quality and the most innovative work and to take firm steps forward within the framework of trust. My biggest goal is to be recognized as a quality and reliable software developer in the market and to work by removing borders in cooperation with different countries.
